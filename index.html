Privacy & cookies policy

Privacy

This website uses the Google Firebase ‘Realtime database’ service, to store GIS data and Asset survey condition data, and the Firebase ‘storage’ service to store photos captured during mapping and during Asset condition surveying . The Firebase server stores the date of the most recent login for each user. For further information see firebase.com/terms

Cookie policy

The website uses cookies to store information about your browser, and your interactions with the website. Cookies, are small text files that are stored on your computer, and accessible only to the websites which create them.

This website uses 'Google Analytics' and 'Mapbox' cookies. The information we get from Google Analytics cookies helps us ensure this website meets your needs and to help us improve it. This information gathered is compiled on an aggregate, anonymous basis. Further information about Google Analytics privacy policy may be obtained from google.com/privacy

‘Mapbox’ cookies are used by the Mapbox mapping services to track user interactions with the Mapbox API, and which does include collecting the User's IP address, and retaining it for a fixed amount of time. For more information on Mapbox privacy policy please see: mapbox.com
